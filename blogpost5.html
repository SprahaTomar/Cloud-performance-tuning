Big Data

AB devillers
30 August

Big data refers to the immense volume, variety, and velocity of data generated in today's digital world. This data can come from various sources, including social media, sensors, and transaction records. Big data technologies, such as Hadoop and Spark, enable the storage, processing, and analysis of these massive datasets to extract valuable insights.Big data refers to the massive volumes of structured and unstructured data that organizations collect and analyze to gain insights and make data-driven decisions. It involves handling data sets too large and complex for traditional data processing methods. Big data technologies like Hadoop and Spark enable storage, processing, and analysis of such data for valuable business insights.
