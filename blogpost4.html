Cyber Security and forenscins

Chris Gayle
05 November

Cybersecurity involves protecting computer systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, cyberattacks, and data breaches. Forensics, in the context of cybersecurity, refers to the process of investigating and analyzing cyber incidents to identify and mitigate security breaches. These areas are critical for safeguarding sensitive information and ensuring the integrity of digital environments. Cybersecurity: Cybersecurity encompasses a wide range of practices and technologies designed to protect computer systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, cyberattacks, and data breaches. This includes tools like firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and encryption protocols. Cybersecurity Forensics: In the event of a security breach, cybersecurity forensics involves investigating and analyzing the incident to determine how it occurred and its impact. This helps organizations improve their security measures and may also be used for legal purposes.
